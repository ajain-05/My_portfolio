About

Shree Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi, is the Governing and Administrative body of Shri
Saibaba's Samadhi Temple and all others temples in this premises, and devoted towards
teachings of Saibaba "Sabka Malik Ek" and providing amenities to Sai devotees who are
visiting Shirdi and Sai Samadhi Temple.
