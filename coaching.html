Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  1. Which age groups do we assist?
    • We assist to students from Class-1 through Class-10, providing tailored coaching to meet the unique needs of each age group.Our approach ensure that students at all levels receive the support and guidance they need to succeed.
  2. How can I schedule a session?
    • You can schedule a session by contacting me directly through the contact form on our website or by calling me through the number at the Home Page.
  3. What subjects do you offer tutoring in?
    • We offer tutoring in a variety of subjects, including Mathematics, Science, English, and more. We customize the curriculum to align with the school syllabus and the individual goals of each student.
  4. What is your cancellation policy?
    • If you need to cancel or reschedule a session, please inform us at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to accommodate other students and ensure everyone gets the support they need. Late cancellations may incur a fee, which will be discussed during our initial consultation.
